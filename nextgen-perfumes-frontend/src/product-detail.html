About Velvet Rose

Velvet Rose is a masterpiece of perfumery, crafted for the modern woman who appreciates timeless elegance. This sophisticated fragrance opens with sparkling bergamot and fresh pear, leading to a heart of exquisite Bulgarian rose and jasmine. The base notes of sandalwood, vanilla, and white musk create a lasting impression that's both romantic and contemporary.

Perfect for both day and evening wear, Velvet Rose embodies femininity and grace. Each bottle is carefully crafted with premium ingredients sourced from the finest perfume houses in France, ensuring exceptional quality and longevity.
